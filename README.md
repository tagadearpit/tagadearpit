## 🪄 A Spark of Me

<img align="right" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/3n3a/3n3a/main/wave.gif" height="45" />

Hi! I'm **Arpit Tagade** — a passionate Full-Stack AI Engineer who builds at the intersection of elegant design and powerful technology. I'm the mind behind **[Monika-AI](https://github.com/tagadearpit/Monika-AI)**, **[Neosis](https://github.com/tagadearpit/Neosis)**, and more.

Welcome to my digital playground, where every line of code tells a story:

- 🎨 I craft **stunning UIs** paired with **robust backend logic**
- 🧠 I build bespoke **AI assistants** and conversational experiences
- 🚀 I explore the bleeding edge of what technology can do
- ✨ I chase "wow" moments — interactivity, animation, and bold ideas
- 🤝 I love collaborating, sharing knowledge, and lifting others up

> **TL;DR:** AI. Full-stack magic. Fearless creativity. You're in the right place. 🚀
